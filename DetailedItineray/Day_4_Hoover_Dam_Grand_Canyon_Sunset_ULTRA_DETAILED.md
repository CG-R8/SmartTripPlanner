## 10:45 AM-12:00 PM - HOOVER DAM VISIT

### **⭐ HOOVER DAM - ENGINEERING MARVEL ⭐**

**Quick Facts:**
- Built: 1931-1936 (during Great Depression!)
- Height: 726 feet (70 stories!)
- Length: 1,244 feet across
- Thickness: 660 feet thick at base
- Concrete: 3.25 million cubic yards
- Workers: 21,000 workers, 96 deaths during construction
- Purpose: Flood control, power generation, water supply
- Powers: Las Vegas + much of Southwest
- Creates: Lake Mead (largest reservoir in US)
- **Absolutely incredible piece of engineering!**
