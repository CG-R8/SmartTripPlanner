### **IN-FLIGHT: Spirit NK 1670 (6:59 PM EST - 10:14 PM PST)**

**Flight Duration:** 5 hours 15 minutes  
**Time Change:** Lose 3 hours (land at 10:14 PM PST = 1:14 AM EST body time)  
**Distance:** ~2,800 miles

**Seat Assignments:**
- Spirit doesn't assign seats for free (pay $5-50 for selection)
- If you didn't pre-select: You'll get assigned at check-in
- **With toddler:** Try to get window + middle + aisle (3-seat row)
  - Toddler at window (they can look out)
  - Parent in middle
  - Parent in aisle (easier bathroom access)

**Spirit Airlines - What to Expect:**
- **Budget airline:** No frills, basic service
- **Seats:** Don't recline much, firm cushions
- **Legroom:** 28-inch pitch (tight for tall adults)
- **No free snacks/drinks:** Everything costs money
- **No entertainment:** No seat-back screens
  - Bring your own! (Tablet with downloaded shows)
- **Wi-Fi:** Available for purchase ($8-10)
  - Usually slow, skip it and use downloaded content

**Flight Timeline & Toddler Management:**

**6:59-7:30 PM (Takeoff + Climb):**
- Plane taxis, takes off
- **Ear pressure relief for toddler:**
  - Give them something to suck: sippy cup, pacifier, lollipop
  - Or chew: gummy bears, soft candy
  - Swallowing helps equalize ear pressure
  - If they cry, that's actually GOOD (opens eustachian tubes)
- Fasten seatbelts
- Toddler sits in their seat (lap or own seat if you bought one)
- Climb to cruising altitude (30,000+ feet)

**7:30-9:30 PM (Mid-Flight - 2 Hours):**
- This is your "main entertainment window"
- **Toddler activities:**
  - **Hour 1:** Tablet time - let them watch favorite shows
    - Downloaded shows: No Wi-Fi needed!
    - Headphones: Essential (don't bother other passengers)
    - Brightness: Lower it to save battery
  - **Hour 2:** Snack time + quiet activities
    - Snacks: Goldfish, fruit pouches, crackers
    - Activities: Coloring book, stickers, small toys
    - Book reading: Look at picture books together
- **Parent activities:**
  - One parent can nap if toddler occupied
  - Read, listen to music, relax
  - Stretch legs: Walk to bathroom, back of plane

**9:30-10:14 PM (Final Hour + Descent):**
- **If toddler falls asleep:** AMAZING! Let them sleep!
  - They'll wake up for landing
  - Sleep = easier flight
- **If toddler still awake:**
  - More tablet time okay
  - Look out window (if window seat) - see city lights
  - Talk about "we're going to California!"
- **Descent begins ~9:45 PM:**
  - Ears will pop again
  - Same pressure relief tactics: suck, chew, swallow
  - Toddler might cry - that's normal
- **Flight attendants:**
  - Collect any trash
  - Prepare cabin for landing
- **Landing:** Watch out window - see LA at night!

**Spirit In-Flight Service:**
- No free drinks/snacks
- If you want something:
  - Water: $3
  - Soda: $3-4
  - Snacks: $3-6
  - **Recommendation:** Bring your own snacks! Save money.

**Bathroom Strategy:**
- Airplane bathrooms are TINY
- Go before toddler needs emergency
- Change diaper at seat if possible (lay changing pad on your lap)
- If must use bathroom:
  - Take toddler even if they "don't have to go"
  - Prevents emergency 15 minutes later
  - One parent goes with toddler, other watches bags
